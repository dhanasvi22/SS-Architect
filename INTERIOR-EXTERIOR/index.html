Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: What is the difference between interior design and architecture?

Interior design focuses on making indoor spaces functional and beautiful, while architecture deals with the overall structure and planning of the building.

Q2: How long does the design process take?

The timeline depends on project size. A single room may take 2–3 weeks; a full house or office can take 2–6 months.

Q3: Do you provide customized designs?

Yes — we create fully customized designs based on the client's preferences, lifestyle, and budget.

Q4: How is the budget decided?

Budget depends on area size, material quality, furniture, and design complexity. We provide an estimated cost sheet before starting.

Q5: Do you provide 3D designs and virtual tours?

Yes — we offer 3D views and virtual walkthroughs so clients can visualize the final look before execution.

Q6: What is included in exterior design?

Exterior design includes façade design, landscaping, lighting, outdoor furniture, terrace and balcony planning.

Q7: Can clients share their own ideas or reference photos?

Of course — we always consider client suggestions to add a personal touch to the design.

Q8: Do you provide maintenance and after-service support?

Yes — we offer after-project support, including minor modifications and maintenance guidance.

Q9: Do you also design small spaces like 1BHK apartments?

Yes — we specialize in maximizing small spaces with smart and creative interior solutions.

Q10: How does the consultation process work?

We first understand requirements and budget, then propose design ideas and layouts. Execution begins after final approval.
